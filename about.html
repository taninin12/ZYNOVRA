About the Campaign

About the Vaccination Drive

The Zynovra Global HPV Vaccination Drive is a global public health initiative conducted by Zynovra Biopharmaceuticals to improve access to HPV vaccination in participating regions. The campaign will be held from 1 September 2026 to 12 September 2026 across vaccination hubs in India, the United States, Europe, China, Africa, Iran, and Lebanon.

The initiative aims to increase awareness about Human Papillomavirus (HPV), encourage early vaccination, and improve access to preventive healthcare for individuals aged 14 years and above.

About Zynovra Biopharmaceuticals

Zynovra Biopharmaceuticals is a fictional biotechnology company created for this academic project with a focus on vaccine innovation, preventive healthcare, and improving global access to immunization.

Through research-driven innovation, Zynovra aims to develop affordable and effective vaccines while collaborating with healthcare professionals and public health organizations to strengthen disease prevention.

About PAPPISHIELD®

PAPPISHIELD® is a prophylactic Human Papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine developed to help protect against HPV infections associated with cervical cancer and other HPV-related diseases.

The vaccine is intended for individuals aged 14 years and above and is administered by trained healthcare professionals at authorized vaccination hubs.

Eligibility Criteria

  • ✅ Individuals aged 14 years and above
  • ✅ Must provide a valid Government-issued Identification.
  • ✅ Individuals with severe allergies or underlying medical conditions should consult a healthcare professional before vaccination.
  • ✅ Pregnant individuals should seek medical advice before receiving the vaccine.
  • ✅ Vaccination is administered only at authorized Zynovra Vaccination Hubs.
