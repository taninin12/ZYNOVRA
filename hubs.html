What to Bring

Please carry the following documents when visiting your selected vaccination hub.

Government ID

Passport, Aadhaar Card, National ID, or any valid government-issued photo identification.

Registration ID

Carry your Registration ID and Appointment Confirmation (printed or digital).

Payment

Carry the applicable vaccination fee or proof of payment, if required.

Important Guidelines

  • ✔ Arrive at least 15 minutes before your appointment.
  • ✔ Carry a valid Government ID.
  • ✔ Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to your upper arm.
  • ✔ Inform the healthcare professional about any allergies or medical conditions.
  • ✔ Remain under observation for 15–30 minutes after vaccination.
